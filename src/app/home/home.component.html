What is hydroponics ?

Hydroponics is a method of growing plants without soil. Instead, plants are grown in a nutrient-rich water solution, which provides all the essential minerals they need to thrive

Key Features of Hydroponics:

    Nutrient Control: You have precise control over the nutrients and pH levels, which can optimize plant growth.
    Water Efficiency: It uses significantly less water than traditional soil-based gardening since the water is recirculated.
    Space Efficiency: Plants can be grown closer together, making hydroponics ideal for urban or indoor farming.
    Growth: Plants often grow faster because they receive nutrients more efficiently and in ideal conditions.
